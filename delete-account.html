Delete your account

Last updated: 16 June 2026

BookDNA (developed by Mohan Krishna G R) lets you delete your account and all associated data at any time.

How to request deletion

Email mohankrishnagr08@gmail.com from the email address linked to your BookDNA account, with the subject Delete my account. We verify the request and process it within 30 days.

If a “Delete account” option is available in the app (Settings → Account), you can also use that to delete your account directly.

What is deleted

When your account is deleted, we permanently remove the data associated with it:

  • Your profile (name, email, avatar)
  • Your library (books, covers, prices, ratings)
  • Reading sessions and reading history
  • Notes, lends, and reading goals
  • AI chat history and analyses
  • Notification/device tokens and premium/entitlement records

What is kept

  • Anonymized, aggregated analytics and crash diagnostics that are not linked to your account.
  • Information we are required to retain by law, kept no longer than the law requires.

Retention period

Account data is deleted within 30 days of a verified request. Residual copies in encrypted backups are purged within a further 30 days.
